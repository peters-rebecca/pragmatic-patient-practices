Cyclical Thinking Companions™ (CTCs):

The Cyclical Thinking Companions™ are a solution to your cyclical thinking problems. We all worry about things on repeat, but now you don't have to. These audio backpacks are available for individual purchase, but they work best at events. Rent them for you next party and see how much good can be done when friends learn together!

CTC: Forgiveness deals specifically with the idea of forgiveness. If we don’t forgive others, how can we expect to be forgiven for things we do wrong?
